    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>By Default they are actually TSV (tab separated) which you can change by setting </p>

<pre><code>  mapred.textoutputformat.separator=","
</code></pre>

<p>and then you can download it from HDFS either by hdfs -get command , or using -merge command to get a merged output of all the part-ooo* files in a single local file</p>

<pre><code>  hadoop fs -get hdfs://nn.example.com/user/hadoop/file localfile
</code></pre>

<p>or </p>

<pre><code>  Usage: hadoop fs -getmerge &lt;src&gt; &lt;localdst&gt; [addnl]
</code></pre>
