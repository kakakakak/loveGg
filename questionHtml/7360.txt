<p>There are several open source options for stream processing in the meantime, e.g. Apache Storm, Apache Spark or Apache Samza.</p>

<p>However, they do not have the "things you found very useful" with StreamBase, probably. StreamBase has powerful but easy tooling for development, testing, deployment and monitoring.</p>
