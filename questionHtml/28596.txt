<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>All the outout files produced by the reducers already have unique names part-r-00001 and such.
There is a partition number you can read in case you need that number from your code.</p>

<p>Centralized counters that must be guaranteed unique break a lot of the scalability of Hadoop.</p>

<p>So if you need something different then I would use something like a Sha1 of the task id of the reducer to get something that is unique over multiple jobs.</p>
