<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>As it seems from your commands history, you can replace <code>hadoop</code> by <code>/usr/local/hadoop/bin/hadoop</code> and it should help.</p>

<hr>

<p>If you want to use <code>hadoop</code> command without specifying the full path to it, you can edit <code>~/.bashrc</code> file and add the following line:</p>

<pre><code>export PATH=$PATH:/usr/local/hadoop/bin/
</code></pre>

<p>Then you need to reopen your terminal.</p>
