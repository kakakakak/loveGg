    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If your primary objective is to learn Hadoop then it does not matter you learn it on Windows or Linux, because everything is exactly same on both platforms. I have extensively used Hadoop on both platform and found all the commands and processing are identical on Windows and Linux. So here are my suggestions:</p>

<ol>
<li>Download VMware VMPlayer on your Windows/Linux Machine </li>
<li><p>Download CDH Virtual Machine for VMware</p>

<p><a href="https://ccp.cloudera.com/display/SUPPORT/Downloads" rel="nofollow">https://ccp.cloudera.com/display/SUPPORT/Downloads</a></p></li>
</ol>

<p>Access virtual machine in your Windows/Linux box and follow the tutorials exactly they are on Linux.</p>

<p>Same info is shared here: </p>

<p><a href="http://stackoverflow.com/questions/10310736/hadoop-on-windows/10310828#10310828">Hadoop on Windows</a></p>
