<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Yes, you can specify the <code>mongo.input.fields</code> option while configuring the MongoDB Hadoop Connector:</p>

<pre><code>val config = new Configuration()
config.set("mongo.input.uri", [Your URI])
config.set("mongo.input.query", [Your Query])
config.set("mongo.input.fields", "{column: 1}")
</code></pre>

<p>Refer to <a href="http://docs.mongodb.org/master/tutorial/project-fields-from-query-results/" rel="nofollow">the official MongoDB Documentations</a> for the projection's syntax.</p>
