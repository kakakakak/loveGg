<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Few suggestions first. You don't actually need to put <strong>dfs.replication</strong>, <strong>mapred.job.tracker</strong> in core-site.xml and <strong>dfs.support.append</strong> in hbase-site.xml files. It's not required.</p>

<p>Please make sure NN is running fine and is out of safemode. Also, it's better to turn IPv6 off and add <strong>hbase.zookeeper.property.dataDir</strong> and <strong>hbase.zookeeper.property.clientPort</strong> in hbase-site.xml file and set export <strong>HBASE_MANAGES_ZK</strong> in hbase-env.sh to <strong>true</strong>. Restart HBase after changing config files.</p>
