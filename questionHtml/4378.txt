<p>I found the solution : it was some jar collisions. I had duplicate jar files both in <code>hadoop-x.y.z/</code> and <code>hadoop-x.y.z/lib and in path-to-java/jre/lib/ext/</code>.</p>

<p>I just removed the formers and everything works fine again.</p>
