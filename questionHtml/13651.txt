<p>Apparently it was a matter of <strong>SolrJ</strong> version, the value of <code>zkHostString</code>, and using the class CloudSolrServer.</p>

<p>When I switched to SolrJ 4.10.3 and used the following section:</p>

<pre><code>CloudSolrServer solr
String zkHostString = "localhost:9983";
solr = new CloudSolrServer(zkHostString);
solr.setDefaultCollection("collection1");
</code></pre>

<p>It worked fine and was able to insert a sample document into Solr for indexing.</p>
