<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Modify your hdfs-site.xml</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;dfs.name.dir&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;/home/user_to_run_hadoop/hdfs/name&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;

&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;dfs.data.dir&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;/home/user_to_run_hadoop/hdfs/data&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Make sure to create directory <code>hdfs</code> at <code>/home/user_to_run_hadoop</code>. Then create 2 directories <code>name</code> and <code>data</code> on <code>hdfs</code></p>

<p>After that you need to <code>chmod -R 755 ./hdfs/</code> and <code>path_to_hadoop_home/bin/hadoop namenode -format</code></p>
