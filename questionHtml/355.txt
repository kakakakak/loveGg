<p>Its seems that your Hadoop installation contain multiple bindings for slf4j, removing one of the binding might solve the problem. 
Add following exclusion in the dependencies that caused conflict.</p>

<pre><code>&lt;exclusions&gt;
    &lt;exclusion&gt;
        &lt;groupId&gt;org.slf4j&lt;/groupId&gt;
        &lt;artifactId&gt;slf4j-log4j12&lt;/artifactId&gt;
    &lt;/exclusion&gt;
&lt;/exclusions&gt;
