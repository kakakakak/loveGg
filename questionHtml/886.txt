<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Go to the <a href="http://hadoop.apache.org/" rel="nofollow">home page</a> of the project, read about it as much as you can, set it up on you local machine. Follow instructions from <a href="http://www.michael-noll.com/tutorials/running-hadoop-on-ubuntu-linux-single-node-cluster/" rel="nofollow">this site</a> for setting it up.
