<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The problem is with the filename you are using "~/ayush/output/part-00000" relies on Unix shell (sh, bash, ksh) tilde expansion to replace the "~" with the pathname of your home directory.</p>

<p>Java (and C, and C++, and most other programming languages) don't do tilde expansion.  You need to provide the pathname as "/home/ayush/output/part-00000" ... or whatever absolute pathname it is that the tilded form expands to.  </p>

<p>Strictly speaking, the URI should be created as follows:</p>

<pre><code>new File("/home/ayush/output/part-00000").toURI()
</code></pre>

<p><strong>not</strong> as</p>

<pre><code>new URI("/home/ayush/output/part-00000")
</code></pre>

<p>The latter creates a URI without a "protocol", and that could be problematic. </p>
