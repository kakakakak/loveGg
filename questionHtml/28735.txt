<pre><code>ERROR [main]: exec.Task (SessionState.java:printError(833)) - Job Submission failed with exception 'java.lang.RuntimeException(java.io.IOException: Merging of credentials not supported in this version of hadoop)'

    at org.apache.hadoop.hive.ql.plan.PlanUtils.configureJobConf(PlanUtils.java:856)
    at org.apache.hadoop.hive.ql.plan.MapWork.configureJobConf(MapWork.java:540)
</code></pre>

<p>I have changed version of hive to 0.14 but same error.
What is the solution of it?
Note: I cannot upgrade hadoop.</p>

<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Although your version of Hive is current, this is not the source of your error. You need to upgrade your Hadoop version, to 2.4.0 or above.</p>

<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Its simplest solution is to change hadoop 2.4. According to your comments you think apache Nutch could not work for hadoop 2.4. This problem is solved in <a href="http://stackoverflow.com/questions/23436168/nutch-in-hadoop-2-x">this question</a>. Basically, at compile time, change hadoop version number in Nutch. You can also use hadoop version 2.5 with Nutch. Follow <a href="https://github.com/dobromyslov/hbase/releases/tag/0.94.24-hadoop-2.5.0" rel="nofollow">this</a> for guide. </p>

<p>Also review this answer from mailing <a href="https://mail-archives.apache.org/mod_mbox/nutch-user/201410.mbox/%3CCA+sBHJb2ANfkVwJPEyRPRe=e6kogS9+Y_LJ_PS1V4CXFsEWABQ@mail.gmail.com%3E" rel="nofollow">list</a>. According to latest information from <a href="http://nutch.apache.org/" rel="nofollow">http://nutch.apache.org/</a>. It support hadoop 2.4</p>
