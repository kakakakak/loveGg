    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>i met same issue, it is becasue hadoop 1.0.x has a difference need config two variables in hadoop_env.sh:</p>

<p>export HADOOP_HEAPSIZE=2000</p>

<h1>Extra Java runtime options.  Empty by default.</h1>

<h1>export HADOOP_OPTS=-server</h1>

<p>export HADOOP_OPTS="-Djava.security.krb5.realm=OX.AC.UK -Djava.security.krb5.kdc=kdc0.ox.ac.uk:kdc1.ox.ac.uk"</p>

<p>when you add these two variables, you will not see any error or warning during starting daemons, so that you can resolve your issue. good luck!</p>
