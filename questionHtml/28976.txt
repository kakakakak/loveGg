<p>I've managed to get this working to the point where jobs are dispatched, tasks executed, and results compiled.</p>

<ul>

<p>However we still need to get the servlets to understand cygwin symlinks.  I have no idea how to do this in Jetty.</p>

<p>These two links show how to allow Tomcat and jetty to follow symlinks, but I don't know if this works in cygwin.
*  <a href="http://www.lamoree.com/machblog/index.cfm?event=showEntry&amp;entryId=A2F0ED76-A500-41A6-A1DFDE0D1996F925" rel="nofollow">http://www.lamoree.com/machblog/index.cfm?event=showEntry&amp;entryId=A2F0ED76-A500-41A6-A1DFDE0D1996F925</a>

<p>Otherwise we'll have to open up the jetty code and replace java.io.File with org.apache.hadoop.fs.LinkedFile.</p>
