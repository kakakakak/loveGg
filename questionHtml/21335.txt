<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Don't panic! Your command is incorrect.</p>

<pre><code>ssh -vvv localhost -p 8047
</code></pre>

<p>The parameter <code>-p 8047</code> means to reach port 8047, however SSH daemon runs at port 22. The config of sshd you pasted, has already proven my assumption. </p>

<p>You can try to access localhost via SSH by using</p>

<pre><code>ssh hduser@localhost
</code></pre>
