    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The problem is that the JARs are built into the assembly JARs when you run</p>

<p>sbt/sbt clean assembly</p>

<p>What I did is:
sbt/sbt clean package</p>

<p>This will only give you the small JARs. The next steps is to update the CLASSPATH in the 
bin/compute-classpath.sh script manually, appending all the JARs.</p>

<p>With :</p>

<p>sbt/sbt assembly</p>

<p>We can't introduce our own Hadoop patch since it will always pull from Maven repo, unless we 
hijack the repository path, or do a 'mvn install' locally. This is more of a hack I think.</p>
