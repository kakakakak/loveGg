<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Checking the full task log of the failed attempt <code>attempt_1415950730849_0001_m_000000_0</code> will help tell why you ran into the given exception.</p>

<p>The most common reason of observing such an error is a misconfigured value of <code>io.sort.mb</code> in your job. Its value must never be anywhere close to (or higher than) the configured map task heap size, and must also not currently exceed <code>~2000 MB</code> (Java array maximum size).</p>

<p>An upstream improvement of making the error more clear on the true failure was also filed and resolved recently, via <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/MAPREDUCE-6194" rel="nofollow">MAPREDUCE-6194</a>.</p>
