    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You've specified the hadoop jars as dependencies with a scope of <code>&lt;scope&gt;provided&lt;/scope&gt;</code>.<br>
This implies that they will be on the classpath at runtime, if you want them to be included in your jar, then remove the scope tags and accept the default scope of <strong>compile</strong>.  </p>

<p>see <a href="http://maven.apache.org/pom.html" rel="nofollow">http://maven.apache.org/pom.html</a></p>
