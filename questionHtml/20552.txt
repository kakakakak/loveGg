<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The client calculates the split informations and writes them with the split info to HDFS. </p>

<p>You can have a look into Hadoop 1.x <code>JobSplit.SplitMetaInfo</code> there is the serialization implemented that also serializes the locations. </p>

<p>The jobtracker just picks up these serializations and schedules them, where the locations are just a hint for faster execution if slots are available. </p>
