    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>As mentioned here:</p>

<p><a href="http://mail-archives.apache.org/mod_mbox/oozie-dev/201210.mbox/%3C507BC0AB.5080201@adition.com%3E" rel="nofollow">http://mail-archives.apache.org/mod_mbox/oozie-dev/201210.mbox/%3C507BC0AB.5080201@adition.com%3E</a></p>

<p>the problems are the permissions with which directories are created.</p>

<p>Setting</p>

<pre><code>umask 022
</code></pre>

<p>in the shell where I run the tests solved the problem for me. 
Before changing umask, in surefire-reports I found:</p>

<pre><code>14/05/14 18:29:25 WARN datanode.DataNode: Invalid directory in dfs.data.dir: 
Incorrect permission for build/test/data/dfs/data/data1, 
expected: rwxr-xr-x, while actual: rwxrwxr-x
</code></pre>
