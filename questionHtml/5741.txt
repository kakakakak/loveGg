<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Basically this error is due to missing atlas jar in oozie share lib.
In HDP the Atlas jar is available in <code>/usr/hdp/2.3.0.0-2557/atlas/</code>
Put all the jars related to atlas in hadoop share lib ..</p>

<pre><code>hadoop fs -put /usr/hdp/2.3.0.0-2557/atlas/hook/hive/* /user/oozie/share/lib/lib200344/hive 
</code></pre>

<p>Add <code>'export HIVE_AUX_JARS_PATH=&lt;atlas package&gt;/hook/hive'</code> in hive-env.sh .<br>
Copy <code>&lt;atlas package&gt;/conf/application.properties</code>to hive conf directory.</p>

<p>Restart the oozie services. This will solve this problem. If anybody face the problem please comment here so that I can help.</p>

<p>[Comment by Immo Huneke: when using the Hortonworks sandbox VM, I found that just putting the jar files in the share/lib folder under HDFS was enough to resolve the problem. I didn't have to update hive-env.sh or copy the application.properties file. But check the exact path of your share/lib folder by executing the command <code>hdfs dfs -ls /user/oozie/share/lib</code> before copying.]</p>
