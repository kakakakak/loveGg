<p>I am not able to append content to an existing file in HDFS.Exception is thrown at the following line.</p>

<pre><code>outputStream = hdfs.append(dirPath);
</code></pre>

<p>where dirPath is "hdfs://master:54310/test/Readme.txt".</p>

<p>Please note that,I am running Hadoop on single node for development.</p>

at org.apache.hadoop.hdfs.server.namenode.FSNamesystem.appendFile(FSNamesystem.java:2347)
