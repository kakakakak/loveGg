    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<pre><code>job.setInputFormatClass(
      org.apache.hadoop.mapreduce.lib.input.FileInputFormat.class);
</code></pre>

<p>You can't use / instantiate the <code>FileInputFormat</code> class: it's an abstract class. </p>

<p>If you want to parse the XML yourself then you'll need to write your own <code>InputFormat</code> that extends <code>FileInputFormat</code>, and the record reader can pass the entire contents to the mapper as the value. I think the <em>Hadoop - The Definitive Guide</em> has an example for <code>WholeFileInputFormat</code>, or something like that, or Google will probably have something:</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.google.com/search?q=wholefileinputformat" rel="nofollow">https://www.google.com/search?q=wholefileinputformat</a></li>
</ul>
