<p>In your case you are doing <strong>input.txt</strong> -> <strong>outseq</strong> -> <strong>ttseq</strong>.</p>

<p>You are using <strong>outputseq</strong> (but not <strong>outseq</strong>) as input to generate <strong>out1</strong>.</p>

<p>And you are getting error with <strong>ttseq</strong>. That is strange? Perhaps you are missing some step in your post.</p>

<p>For me:</p>

<p>This <strong>PASSES</strong>: <strong>text-files</strong> -> <strong>output-seqdir</strong> -> <strong>output-seq2sparse-normalized</strong></p>
