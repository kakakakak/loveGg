<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>One of the way debugging Hadoop in Eclipse is by running hadoop in local mode because map reduce task run in its own JVM.</p>

<p>Also explore <code>MRUnit</code> and <code>Jumbune's debugger</code>.</p>
