<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>It is because it exists on the C:\ drive of the headnode. Simple steps to access it:</p>

<ol>
<li>Go the <a href="http://manage.windowsazure.com" rel="nofollow">manage.windowsazure.com</a> and go to your HDI cluster</li>
<li>Enable remote access, choose a user and a password (note admin is reserved)</li>
<li>Then just login the machine</li>
<li>Open the explorer and go to that path</li>
<li>Either copy&amp;paste to your local machine or open it in notepad</li>
</ol>
