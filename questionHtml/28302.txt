<pre><code> git rebase

<pre><code>15:52:57/spectral.new $git reset HEAD sql/hive-thriftserver/src/main/scala/org/apache/spark/sql/hive/thriftserver/server/SparkSQLOperationManager.scala

M   sql/hive-thriftserver/src/main/scala/org/apache/spark/sql/hive/thriftserver/SparkSQLCLIDriver.scala
</code></pre>

<p>Notice that git diff reports no changes:</p>

<pre><code>5:56:14/spectral.new $git diff sql/hive-thriftserver/src/main/scala/org/apache/spark/sql/hive/thriftserver/server/SparkSQLOperationManager.scala

</code></pre>
