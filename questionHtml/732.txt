<pre><code>sudo -su root spark-submit --executor-memory 512m --num-executors 50 --class com.mycompany.project.SparkHdfsToHBase --master yarn parser-1.0-jar-with-dependencies.jar 0 hdfs://company.com:8020/tmp/testfiles/html_files/* 127.0.0.1 --verbose
</code></pre>

<p>And everything works like a charm. </p>
