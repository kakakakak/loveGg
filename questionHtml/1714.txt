<p>What you should do is to remove the it first.</p>

<pre><code>rm -rf ~/.m2/repository/com/sun/jersey
rm -rf ~/.m2/repository/org/glassfish/packager
rm -rf ~/.m2/repository/jersey
</code></pre>

<p>After that, run the Maven mvn command again.</p>
