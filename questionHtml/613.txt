<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You need to be careful in the way you are detecting the job is done in this way, because there might be output before your job is completely finished.</p>

<p>To answer your direct question, to test for existence I typically do <code>hadoop fs -ls $output | wc -l</code> and then make sure the number is greater than 0.</p>

<p>My suggestion is you use <code>&amp;&amp;</code> to tack on the move:</p>

<pre><code>hadoop ... myjob.jar ... &amp;&amp; hadoop fs -mv $output $new_output &amp;
</code></pre>

<p>This will complete the job, and then perform the move afterwards.</p>
