<p>Besides the <code>Cloudera</code> resources I'd highly recommend you the reference books from <a href="http://oreilly.com/" rel="nofollow">O'Reilly</a> :</p>

<ul>
<li>Hadoop: The Definitive Guide</li>
<li>Programming Pig</li>
<li>Programming Hive </li>
<li>HBase: The Definitive Guide</li>
</ul>

<p>You might also check it's <a href="http://shop.oreilly.com/category/get/data-science-kit.do" rel="nofollow">data science kit</a> as well.</p>
