<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Based on the messsage <code>Connecting to ResourceManager at /0.0.0.0:8030</code>,
are you sure your ResourceManager is supposed to be at 0.0.0.0:8030 (the default)?
If not you should add the following to your <code>yarn-site.xml</code>:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.hostname&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;MASTER ADDRESS&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.resource-tracker.address&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;${yarn.resourcemanager.hostname}:8025&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.scheduler.address&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;${yarn.resourcemanager.hostname}:8030&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.address&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;${yarn.resourcemanager.hostname}:8040&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.webapp.address&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;${yarn.resourcemanager.hostname}:8088&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
&lt;property&gt;
  &lt;name&gt;yarn.resourcemanager.admin.address&lt;/name&gt;
  &lt;value&gt;${yarn.resourcemanager.hostname}:8033&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Replace MASTER ADDRESS with the address of the master node. You can individually change the address of the resource manager's webapp, admin, etc.</p>
