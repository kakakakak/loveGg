<p>Finally, I find the solution. The build error is due to maven generics, which is a popular-google-search issue.
you need to add this to your pom.xml:</p>

<p><div class="snippet" data-lang="js" data-hide="false">
<div class="snippet-code">
<pre class="snippet-code-html lang-html prettyprint-override"><code>&lt;project ...&gt;
  &lt;dependencies&gt;
   ...
  &lt;/dependencies&gt;
  &lt;build&gt;
    &lt;plugins&gt;
       &lt;plugin&gt;
           &lt;groupId&gt;org.apache.maven.plugins&lt;/groupId&gt;
           &lt;artifactId&gt;maven-compiler-plugin&lt;/artifactId&gt;
           &lt;version&gt;2.3.1&lt;/version&gt;
           &lt;configuration&gt;
               &lt;source&gt;1.5&lt;/source&gt;
               &lt;target&gt;1.5&lt;/target&gt;
           &lt;/configuration&gt;
       &lt;/plugin&gt;
    &lt;/plugins&gt;
  &lt;/build&gt;
&lt;/project&gt;</code></pre>
</div>
</div>
</p>

<p>And here is a link: <a href="http://www.mkyong.com/maven/maven-generics-are-not-supported-in-source-1-3/" rel="nofollow">maven generics</a></p>
