<p>We have a database of about 1000 GB (thousand Gigabytes) and we are considering Hadoop to carry out time series analyses. Problem is that Hadoop takes some time to get into and for the size of our database Hadoop is actually even an oversized solution. My question is whether anyone knows a solution similar for Hadoop at a smaller scale. It also needs to have some SQL-like query language. Only thing that comes to my mind is JBoss Infinispan. But I wanted to check out whether there are other known solutions.</p>
