    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Here:</p>

<pre class="lang-html prettyprint-override"><code>&lt;dependency&gt;
    &lt;groupId&gt;javax.servlet&lt;/groupId&gt;
    &lt;artifactId&gt;servlet-api&lt;/artifactId&gt;
    &lt;version&gt;2.5&lt;/version&gt;
&lt;/dependency&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>It should be 3.0 at least since your web.xml specifies <code>version="3.0"</code>. But maintaining this dependency may give you conflicts with Java EE 7.0 dependency here:</p>

<pre class="lang-html prettyprint-override"><code>&lt;dependency&gt;
    &lt;groupId&gt;javax&lt;/groupId&gt;
    &lt;artifactId&gt;javaee-api&lt;/artifactId&gt;
    &lt;version&gt;7.0&lt;/version&gt;
    &lt;scope&gt;provided&lt;/scope&gt;
&lt;/dependency&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Looks like you're just copying/pasting/crossing your fingers and hoping that everything works. This is not how you're supposed to do it. Just import the relevant dependencies you will use.</p>
