<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
 &lt;name&gt;hadoop.tmp.dir&lt;/name&gt;
 &lt;value&gt;/var/lib/hadoop-hdfs/cache/${user.name}&lt;/value&gt;
 &lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>When I made some change, I carelessly changed the value to:</p>

<pre><code>file:///var/lib/hadoop-hdfs/cache/${user.name}
</code></pre>

<p>which caused my problem!</p>
