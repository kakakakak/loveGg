<ol>
<li><p>Stop all hadoop services </p></li>
<li><p>Delete dfs/namenode</p></li>
<li>Delete dfs/datanode from both slaves and masters</li>
<li>Check the premission of the Hadoop folder:


<blockquote>
  <p>sudo chmod –R 755 /usr/local/hadoop</li>
  <li>Restart Hadoop</li>
  <li><p>Check/verify the folder permission.</p></p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
  <p>sudo chmod –R 755 /home/hadoop/appdata</p>
</blockquote></li>
<li>If you still have the problem check the log files </li>
