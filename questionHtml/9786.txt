<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Your problem is related to huge data transfer within the cluster. Apache Hadoop has a specific tool for this purpose called distcp. It enables all the nodes in the cluster to equally contribute to data transfer to HDFS.</p>

<p>Please read more at <a href="http://hadoop.apache.org/docs/r0.20.2/distcp.html" rel="nofollow">http://hadoop.apache.org/docs/r0.20.2/distcp.html</a></p>
