    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The problem was due to a difference between the cluster id of data node (<code>../hadoop-2.5.0/hdfs/datanode/current/VERSION</code>) and the cluster id in the namenode as in <code>../hadoop-2.5.0/hdfs/namenode/current/VERSION</code>. I've just had to set to the same CID, but no idea what caused this difference, may be formatting HDFS many times via <code>$HADOOP_HOME/bin/hdfs namenode -format</code>.</p>
