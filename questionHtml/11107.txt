<p>1) Missed the @Override annotation
2) Imported from import org.apache.hadoop.mapred.InputSplit instead of  org.apache.hadoop.mapreduce.InputSplit;</p>

<h2>CUSTOMRECORDREADER</h2>

<p>1) Imports were done from org.apache.hadoop.mapred.* and not from  org.apache.hadoop.mapreduce.*;</p>
