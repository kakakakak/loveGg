<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Make sure that /etc/hosts.allow contains:</p>

<pre><code>ssh:ALL:allow
sshd:ALL:allow
</code></pre>

<p>OR</p>

<pre><code>ssh:localhost:allow
sshd:localhost:allow
</code></pre>

<p>OR - some other variant</p>

<pre><code>ssh:{host1,host2,host3...}:allow
sshd{host1,host2,host3...}:allow
</code></pre>

<p>INSURE that the first line in the file DOES NOT begin with ALL:ALL:DENY</p>

<p>NOTHING will be able to communicate with the host... on any port.</p>
