<p>Steps how I fixed this problem:</p>

<ol>
<li>I created a directory called wordcount (<code>/home/cloudera/wordcount</code>).</li>
<li>Added <code>WordCount.java</code> to <code>/home/cloudera/wordcount</code> directory.</li>

<pre><code>javac \
-cp /usr/lib/hadoop/\*:/usr/lib/hadoop/client-0.20/\*:/usr/lib/hadoop/lib/\* \
WordCount.java 
-d /home/cloudera/wordcount/wordcount_classes
