    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">

<p>With so many social networks, new services and ubiquitous computing the new age of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Data" rel="nofollow">big data</a> is finally here and is difficult to imagine a future without <a href="http://www.slideshare.net/bigdatalandscape/the-big-data-landscape" rel="nofollow">huge amounts of data</a>. It seems in languages like Java there are popular platforms like <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apache_Hadoop" rel="nofollow">Hadoop</a> with a great ecosystem.</p>

<p>I wonder what is the state of art in Smalltalk. Is anyone doing big data with Smalltalk? Any libraries, applications or experience reports? Recommendations or advices?</p>
