<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>We certainly always welcome new contributors. Although it sounds trite, all open-source projects, Hadoop included, need improved documentation and tests. While this work may not be the sexiest, it does provide new contributors with experience in the code and the opportunity to provide useful patches that committers love to see.  I'd caution against an anti-pattern I often see: announcing or signing up for too much work or suggesting too radical a change before establishing experience and credentials within the project.</p>
