<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>setOutputKeyClass</code> and <code>setOutputValueClass</code> define the types of objects emitted by the reducer, but per your reducer (one) source code those should be <code>Text</code>, <code>Text</code> instead of <code>Text</code>, <code>StockDetailsTuple</code>.</p>

<p>Use <code>setMapOutputKeyClass</code> and <code>setMapOutputValueClass</code> to specify the mapper's output types if they're different to the reducer ones.</p>

<p>PS: your <code>StockReducerOne</code> class modifies instance variables in its <code>reduce</code> method (<code>priceMap</code>, <code>count</code>, ...), which you probably don't want to do. At least you should reset those to initial values at each reducer invokation, otherwise you might get unexpected results.</p>
