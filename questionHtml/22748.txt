    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I have no experience with hadoop or hive, however the following regex will work with what I believe you're looking for.</p>

<p><code>/(\dx\d)(.*)/</code> This will capture/split <code>0x0</code> into the first capture group, and everything afterwards into the second capture group. If you only want the numbers/letters following the 0x0 number (so none of the <code>H123456789</code> or trailing words and letters), use <code>/(\dx\d)([^ ]*)/</code></p>

<p>If I misunderstood what you're looking for, can you just clarify the exact section of that code you provided that you'd like to select and/or capture? Thanks!</p>
