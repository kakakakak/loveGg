<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>This is not a type of question that could be answered as <code>Product1</code> or <code>Product2</code>. It is just too small amount of information given. There is no info about the environment, where the system will run, what type of information will be inserted, how are you going to aggregate it. </p>

<p>The best way is to try:</p>

<ol>
<li>write a test using <code>Product1</code>, </li>
<li>write a the same thing with a <code>Product2</code></li>
<li>start inserting the data which looks as close as possible to the
data you are assuming to get in real environment</li>
<li>make measurements of speed and whatever factors you need</li>
</ol>

<p>and only based on that you will be able to determine what suits you</p>
