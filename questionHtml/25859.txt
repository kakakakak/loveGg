<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>You edited your shell script on a DOS or Windows machine, which uses the CRLF pair (<code>\r\n</code>) instead of the Unix-style LF ('\n') line endings.</p>

<p>The <code>^M</code> is the tell-tale -- it is a <code>\r</code> carriage return character.</p>

<p>This should fix it:</p>

<pre><code>tr -d '\r' &lt; count.sh &gt; count.sh.fixed
mv count.sh.fixed count.sh
</code></pre>

<p>Another option:</p>

<pre><code>sed -i 's/\r//g' count.sh
</code></pre>
