<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Inside your oozie hive action you need to tell oozie where your hive metastore is.</p>

<p>Means you need to pass your hive-site.xml as argument.</p>

<p>Also you need to configure external metastore for hive for it to work. The default derby database configuration will not work for you.</p>

<p>So in simple steps</p>

<p>Create hive settings with external database , say mysql
Pass that hive-site.xml to oozie action</p>

<p>See here for details</p>

<p><a href="http://oozie.apache.org/docs/3.3.1/DG_HiveActionExtension.html" rel="nofollow">http://oozie.apache.org/docs/3.3.1/DG_HiveActionExtension.html</a></p>
