<p>I'm running into this common problem, but none of the answers on similar questions have resolved this. Running single-node on a Windows 2008 R2 VM. The logging when trying to run wordcount is in the following pastebin [1] link. Here is what I've tried:</p>

<ol>
<li>Reformat the name node: deleted namenode and datanode folders, ran hdfs namenode -format</li>
<li>Stopped and restarted yarn and dfs after reformatting. </li>
<li>Confirmed 127.0.0.1 is not in my hosts file.</li>
<li>Over ~200MB of space is available.</li>
<li>Confirmed ResourceManager, NodeManager, DataNode, NameNode and JPS are running</li>
<li>Paths to namenode and datanode folders are correct in hdfs-site.xml</li>
<li>Datanode is running in localhost:50070</li>
<li>Windows Firewall is off.</li>
<li>Confirmed SSH works within cygwin prompt.</li>
</ol>

<p>I'm not sure what to look at next. Any assistance would be greatly appreciated.</p>
