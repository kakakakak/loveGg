    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>So <strong>Result values</strong> instance would contain the full row from the scanner.
To get the appropriate columns from the Result I would do something like :-</p>

<p>VisitorIdVal = value.getColumnLatest(Bytes.toBytes(columnFamily1), Bytes.toBytes("VisitorId"))</p>

<p>friendlistVal = value.getColumnLatest(Bytes.toBytes(columnFamily2), Bytes.toBytes("friendlist"))</p>

<p>Here VisitorIdVal and friendlistVal are of the type <em>keyValue</em> <a href="http://archive.cloudera.com/cdh/3/hbase/apidocs/org/apache/hadoop/hbase/KeyValue.html" rel="nofollow">http://archive.cloudera.com/cdh/3/hbase/apidocs/org/apache/hadoop/hbase/KeyValue.html</a>, to get their values out you can do a <em>Bytes.toString(VisitorIdVal.getValue())</em>
Once you have extracted the values from columns you can check for "VisitorId" in "friendlist"</p>
