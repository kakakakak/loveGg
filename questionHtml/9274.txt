<p>I ran into this problem a couple days ago as well. The newer versions of Hadoop have trouble when it comes to interacting with Windows. You can either switch over to a *nix platform (which you should probably do, almost all of the support for Nutch is aimed at *nix users) or downgrade your version Nutch. The newest version of Nutch which I found to work on Windows Server 2008 was <a href="http://archive.apache.org/dist/nutch/" rel="nofollow">1.2</a>.</p>
