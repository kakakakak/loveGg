<p>This is a Cygwin issue. You need to change all local directories to Cygwin directories, such as <code>c:/cygwin/usr/tmp/hadoop-user/dfs/data</code></p>

<p>Make sure you do this with your temp, logs, and data directories (hdfs-site.xml)</p>
