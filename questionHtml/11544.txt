    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>The problem is that your tasktracker isn't started by the time you try to run the job, it doesn't start up instantly. You can run bin/hadoop job -list-active-trackers to see if the tasktracker is up or not, it may take a moment to finish coming up. No tasktracker = no nodes to map the writer to.</p>
