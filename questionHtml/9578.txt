<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>Looks like a configuration problems.
I'm assuming you use a recent version of Hadoop - if that is the case, you should use the <code>hdfs</code> command instead. So try <code>bin/hdfs dfs -ls</code> to see if any of your fs commands work.
I'm guessing they won't work. In that case you should check your core-site.xml for the HDFS settings (<code>fs.defaultFS</code>).</p>
