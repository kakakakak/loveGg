    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>In all hadoop tutorials, <code>bin/hadoop</code> is the location of hadoop, you could see it also as <code>$HADOOP_HOME/bin/hadoop</code>. <code>$HADOOP_HOME</code> it's where hadoop it's located. In my case, it's in <code>/usr/local/hadoop</code>. But, again, it depends on the instructions that you are following. Check more closely your tutorial !</p>
