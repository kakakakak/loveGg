    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I think you are not using correctly options. </p>

<p>In your <code>mrjob.conf</code> file:</p>

<ol>
<li><strong>mapreduce.output.compress: "true"</strong> means that you want a compressed output</li>
<li><strong>mapreduce.output.compression.codec: org.apache.hadoop.io.compress.SnappyCodec</strong> means that the compression uses Snappy codec</li>
</ol>

<p>You are apparently expecting that your compressed inputs will be correctly read by your mappers. Unfortunately, it does not work like that. If you really want to feed your job with compressed data, you may look at SequenceFile. Another simpler solution would be to feed your job with text files only.</p>

<p><em>[Edit: you should also remove this symbol <code>#</code> at the beginning of lines that define options. Otherwise, they will be ignored]</em></p>
