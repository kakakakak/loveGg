    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I have had this error too when trying to use MRUnit and the maven dependency was not found, so I tried to add it manually. Is this your case? </p>

<p>The <strong>problem</strong> is that if you add the jar manually, it will keep failing asking for the rest of MRUnit jars (the ones in the pom of MRUnit) until you add them all with the same version as in its pom.</p>

<p>The <strong>cause</strong> of mrunit not being found was that I was not using the necessary classifier in the dependecy declaration</p>

<p>The <strong>solution</strong> is <a href="https://cwiki.apache.org/confluence/display/MRUNIT/MRUnit+Tutorial" rel="nofollow">adding the classifier</a> <code>hadoop1</code> or <code>hadoop2</code></p>
