When I start the Ingestion process, the ingestion rate is high but it gradually decreases and I get the following curve.
This curve has been consistent with all my attempts to ingest data.
<img src="http://i.stack.imgur.com/QEU0H.png" alt="This is the curve that I am getting"></p>

<p>I want to know the reason behind this. I have pre-splitting enabled and have it optimized in accordance with my dataset.</p>
