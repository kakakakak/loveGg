<pre><code>select name, some_field1, some_field2
from my_table
where name in (${param_name_filter})
</code></pre>

<p>But it doesn't work.
I tried to change my query (just for testing) and removed a parameter from the query:</p>

<pre><code>select name, some_field1, some_field2
from my_table
where name in ('name 1', 'name 2')
</code></pre>

<p>But it doesn't work either. As a result, I get the message "no data found".
Where did I make a mistake?</p>
