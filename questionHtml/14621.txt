<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>When I face NoClassDefFoundError error with mapreduce, I add jar using one of the jar class in JobBuilder to resolve it.<br>
e.g.<br>
<code>Job job = new Job(conf);
 job.setJarByClass(org.apache.hadoop.hbase.util.Bytes.class);</code></p>

<p>Supply jars using libjars parameter to your job-
e.g.</p>

<pre><code>LIB=hbase-x.x.x.jar
hadoop jar /usr/hdp/2.3.0.0-2557/hbase/lib/hbase-examples.jar org.apache.hadoop.hbase.mapreduce.IndexBuilder -libjars ${LIB}
</code></pre>

<p>you can also add jar to HADOOP_CLASSPATH variable before launch job.</p>
