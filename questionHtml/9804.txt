<p>I have installed HBase recently with Nutch and Solr and had a similar problem. I am not sure if exactly the same and unfortunately I do not have the logs any more but it was definitely connection related.</p>

<p>It turned out that the default connection limit for ZooKeeper was too low and increasing this value in <code>hbase-site.xml</code> solved it for me:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
    &lt;name&gt;hbase.zookeeper.property.maxClientCnxns&lt;/name&gt;
    &lt;value&gt;1000&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>I had the same problem. This answer assumes you work on linux and edited your etc/hosts file accordingly (<a href="http://stackoverflow.com/a/13540883/1607022">http://stackoverflow.com/a/13540883/1607022</a>)</p>

<p>The client port number for zookeeper must be same in configuration files of HBase and whatever you use on top of it.
Solution is; (in my case HBASE 0.90.4 with GORA 0.3) check your hbase-default.xml under your HBASE_HOME/src/main/resources:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;name&gt;hbase.zookeeper.property.clientPort&lt;/name&gt;
&lt;value&gt;2181&lt;/value&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>And there should be a hbase-site.xml under HBql:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
&lt;name&gt;hbase.zookeeper.property.clientPort&lt;/name&gt;
&lt;value&gt;2181&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;description&gt;Property from ZooKeeper's config zoo.cfg.
The port at which the clients will connect.
&lt;/description&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Port numbers should be the same.</p>
