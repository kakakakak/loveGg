<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p><code>Caused by: java.lang.OutOfMemoryError: GC overhead limit exceeded</code> means that you will have to adjust the memory settings of your mapred child, for example:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
    &lt;name&gt;mapred.child.java.opts&lt;/name&gt;
    &lt;value&gt;-Xmx1024m&lt;/value&gt;
&lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>in <code>hadoop/conf/mapred-site.xml</code> or, better yet, in the configuration of your job.</p>

<p>You might have to play with it  a bit to find out what works. Reserving too much memory for the children will also not work. </p>
