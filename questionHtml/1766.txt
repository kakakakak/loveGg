<p>my command looks like: 
hadoop jar grayscalefromfile_exc.jar grayscalefromfile_exc.StubDriver -libjars hipi-0.0.1.jar imgs imgsOut1</p>

<p>where the path looks like:</p>

<pre><code> --

</code></pre>

    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>libjars uploads the given jars to the cluster and then makes them available on the classpath for each mapper / reducer instance</p>

<p>If you want to add additional jars to the driver client classpath, you'll need to use the HADOOP_CLASSPATH env variable:</p>

<pre><code>#&gt; export HADOOP_CLASSPATH=hipi-0.0.1.jar
#&gt; hadoop jar grayscalefromfile_exc.jar grayscalefromfile_exc.StubDriver -libjars hipi-0.0.1.jar imgs imgsOut1
</code></pre>
