<p><strong>Original Content</strong></p>

<p>I have been working hard (<strong>12 hours+</strong>) trying to install elephant-bird locally in my <code>$HOME</code> in a shared research-purpose cluster. after <strong>12 hours+</strong> of hard work, I am still lost in the ocean. Any kind man who cares to pull me up to a boat?</p>

<p>I have strived to install the correct versions of <code>protocol-buffers</code> (version 2.5.0) and <code>thrift</code> (version 0.7.0).</p>

<p>Executing version check gives:</p>

<pre><code>$ protoc --version
libprotoc 2.5.0
$ thrift -version

</code></pre>

<pre><code>git clone git://github.com/kevinweil/elephant-bird.git
</code></pre>

<pre><code>mvn -Dprotobuf.version=2.5.0 -Dmaven.test.skip=true package
</code></pre>
