<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>If you are a maven user and faced this issue - </p>

<pre><code>    &lt;dependency&gt;
        &lt;groupId&gt;org.apache.hadoop&lt;/groupId&gt;
        &lt;artifactId&gt;hadoop-client&lt;/artifactId&gt;
        &lt;version&gt;${hadoop.client.version}&lt;/version&gt;
    &lt;/dependency&gt;
    &lt;dependency&gt;
        &lt;groupId&gt;org.apache.hadoop&lt;/groupId&gt;
        &lt;artifactId&gt;hadoop-common&lt;/artifactId&gt;
        &lt;version&gt;${hadoop.client.version}&lt;/version&gt;
    &lt;/dependency&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>NOTE: <code>:hadoop-client:2.5.2</code> alone didn't bring in all the required hadoop dependencies. That is why I added <code>hadoop-common</code>, which brought all the required deps.</p>
