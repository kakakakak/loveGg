<pre><code>export JAVA_HOME= "/cygdrive/C/Program Files/Java/jdk1.6.0_26"

<p>And the path as <code>%JAVA_HOME%\bin;c:\cygwin\bin;c:\cygwin\usr\sbin</code></p>
