<p>The location (url)</p>

<p><a href="http://www.apache.org/dyn/closer.cgi?path=hadoop/common/hadoop-2.6.0/hadoop-2.6.0.tar.gz" rel="nofollow">http://www.apache.org/dyn/closer.cgi?path=hadoop/common/hadoop-2.6.0/hadoop-2.6.0.tar.gz</a> </p>

<p>Points to a list of mirror sites.</p>

<p>1) Pick one (a Mirror) and use that url; for me that would be</p>

<p><a href="http://mirror.ox.ac.uk/sites/rsync.apache.org/hadoop/common/hadoop-2.6.0/hadoop-2.6.0.tar.gz" rel="nofollow">http://mirror.ox.ac.uk/sites/rsync.apache.org/hadoop/common/hadoop-2.6.0/hadoop-2.6.0.tar.gz</a></p>

<p>I'd also check your location/locale setting with the following command</p>

<pre><code>locale 
</code></pre>

<p>Brew does not ask for the locale to be set in the setup, so I assume its picked up from your locale settings..</p>

<p>Ref <a href="https://github.com/Homebrew/linuxbrew/wiki/Standalone-Installation" rel="nofollow">https://github.com/Homebrew/linuxbrew/wiki/Standalone-Installation</a></p>
