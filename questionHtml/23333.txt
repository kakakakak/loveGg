    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>There seems to be a typo '<strong>utbuntu</strong>' in your original IOException. Can you please check it that's the right hostname or a copy-paste error?</p>

<p>The etc/hosts configs took a bit of trial and error to figure out for a Hadoop 2.2.0 cluster setup but what I did was remove all 127.0.1.1 assignments to the hostname and assigned the actual IP to the machine name and it works. 
e.g.</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>192.168.1.101 ubuntu</p>
</blockquote>

<p>I have a 2-node cluster so my /etc/hosts for master (NameNode) looks like:</p>

<pre><code>127.0.0.1   localhost
#127.0.1.1  myhostname
192.168.1.100   myhostname
192.168.1.100   master
</code></pre>

<p>And /usr/local/hadoop/etc/hadoop/core-site.xml has the following:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;property&gt;
   &lt;name&gt;fs.default.name&lt;/name&gt;
   &lt;value&gt;hdfs://master:9000&lt;/value&gt;
 &lt;/property&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>The main thing to note is that I've commented out the myhostname to 127.0.1.1 association.</p>
